# Vision

The ultimate goal of TAPPaaS is enabling **digital sovereignty** - giving individuals and organizations true ownership and control over their digital infrastructure.

---

## Who Benefits from TAPPaaS?

TAPPaaS serves those who need digital independence but lack the resources for traditional IT infrastructure:

### Families and Communities

Those prioritizing data privacy and independence from big tech platforms. Keep your family photos, documents, and communications under your own control.

### Small-to-Medium Businesses

Organizations needing reliable office infrastructure without the cost of a dedicated IT department. Run your business on infrastructure you own and control.

### Organizations Requiring Local Resilience

Government agencies, NGOs, and critical infrastructure providers who need systems that work even when internet connectivity is limited or unavailable.

---

## Platform Democracy

Through community-driven development and a commitment to open source, TAPPaaS delivers the tools needed to break free from dependency on hyperscaler platforms.

!!!tip "Platform Democracy"
    TAPPaaS operates on the principle of "Platform Democracy" - where producers are also consumers of the system. We use what we build, and we build what we need.
