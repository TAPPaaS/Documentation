# About TAPPaaS

TAPPaaS (Trusted Automated Private Platform as a Service) is an open-source platform designed to simplify deploying and managing self-hosted applications on Proxmox virtualization.

## Mission

Our mission is to enable digital sovereignty by providing a simple, powerful, and open platform that anyone can run on their own hardware.

## Goals

- **Simplicity** - Abstract away infrastructure complexity
- **Security** - Built-in firewall, identity management, and network segmentation
- **Resilience** - Automated backup and disaster recovery
- **Privacy** - Your data stays on your hardware

## Project History

TAPPaaS was founded by Lars Rossen and Erik van Busschbach to address the challenges of internet centralization and IT complexity facing families, small businesses, and organizations.

## Governance

TAPPaaS is developed openly on GitHub. Major decisions are made through community discussion and consensus.
